FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for vehicles on GoCarDeal? +
Use our search bar at the top of the homepage or visit the Shop Vehicles page. You can filter by make, model, year, price range, mileage, fuel type, transmission, and location. Narrow your search to Canada, USA, or India depending on your location.
Are all dealers on GoCarDeal verified? +
Yes. Every dealer on GoCarDeal undergoes a comprehensive verification process including license verification, background checks, business registration confirmation, and ongoing customer review monitoring. Look for the green "Verified" badge on any listing.
How does the "Book Test Drive" feature work? +
Click "Book Test Drive" on any listing. Fill in your contact details and preferred date/time. The dealer will confirm within 2 hours via email and phone. All test drives are arranged directly with the dealer at their location.
Can I buy a car from another country? +
Cross-border vehicle purchases are possible in some cases. Our team can assist with import/export documentation, duties, and compliance. Contact our support team for guidance on cross-border purchases between Canada, USA, and India.
Does GoCarDeal charge buyers any fees? +
Absolutely not. GoCarDeal is completely free for buyers. We earn revenue through dealer subscriptions and premium listing placements — never from buyers.
How do I sell my car on GoCarDeal? +
Visit the Sell / Trade page and complete our vehicle submission form. Upload photos, set your price, and submit. Your listing will be sent to verified dealers in your area who may offer to purchase your vehicle.
How quickly can I sell my car? +
Most sellers receive dealer interest within 24–48 hours. Many complete their sale within 1 week. Vehicles with complete photos, accurate descriptions, and competitive pricing sell fastest.
Is it free to list my vehicle? +
Yes, listing your vehicle is 100% free. There are no fees for private sellers. You can upgrade to a Featured Listing for additional exposure, but the standard listing is always free.
How do I become a GoCarDeal dealer partner? +
Visit our Dealer Pricing page and select a subscription plan. Our onboarding team will contact you within 24 hours to complete verification, set up your dealer dashboard, and help you upload your first listings.
What is included in a dealer subscription? +
Dealer subscriptions include a dedicated dealer profile page, vehicle listing slots, lead management dashboard, analytics, priority customer support, and access to our lead generation network across all three countries.
How are dealer leads generated? +
Leads are generated when buyers click "Contact Dealer," "Get Best Price," or "Book Test Drive" on your listings. All leads are delivered instantly to your dealer dashboard with buyer contact information, vehicle interest, and budget details.
What interest rates does GoCarDeal Finance offer? +
Rates start from 3.9% APR for well-qualified buyers. Your rate depends on credit score, loan term, down payment, and vehicle age. Use our calculator to estimate your payment before applying.
Does checking my rate affect my credit score? +
No. Our initial rate check uses a soft credit inquiry that does not affect your credit score. A hard inquiry is only performed if you proceed with a full application.
What currencies are supported for financing? +
We support Canadian Dollars (CAD), US Dollars (USD), and Indian Rupees (INR) through our regional lending partners.
